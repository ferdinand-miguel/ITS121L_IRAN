Services

Palay Procurement

We buy palay directly from farmers at competitive prices, ensuring fair compensation for their hard work and dedication. We offer free pickups for a minimum of 50 bags. Our truck scale provides convenience for farmers to accurately weigh their palay bags before selling to us.

Rice Sales

We offer a variety of high-quality rice varieties, carefully selected to meet the diverse preferences of our customers. From premium Jasmine rice to nutritious brown rice, we have something for everyone.

Agricultural Supplies

Enhance your crop yields with our range of fertilizers and pesticides, sourced from trusted manufacturers and tailored to the needs of Philippine agriculture.

Farm Services

Utilize our modern tractor equipment for efficient land preparation, saving you time and labor during planting season. Maximize efficiency and minimize losses with our Rice/Corn Combine Harvester, designed to streamline the harvesting process.

Delivery Services

Enjoy the convenience of doorstep delivery when you order rice from us. We ensure prompt and reliable delivery to your location, so you can focus on what matters most—growing your business.
